Life Health and Wellness Health Care

Our company is based on the belief that our customers' needs are of the utmost importance. Our entire team of Nurse Practitioners are committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals.
Life Health and Wellness Health Care Medical Clinic began in Phoenix in 2003 to serve the health care needs of the Community. Our Veteran contracts allow us to serve Veterans, Active duty Military, Reservist and their families. We also serve our community, any persons in need of health care. Our current location is 6605 N 19th Avenue in Phoenix. This location has two health care providers. Life Health and Wellness Health Care providers also manages the Wellness Center at the Phoenix Dream Center located at 3210 NW Grand ave in Phoenix. All services are provided at each facility. Our facility provides the best care possible while maintaining HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accessibility Act) compliance for our patientâ€™s electronic health records.

Dr. Beverly Thomas-Carter has been caring for patients in the medical field for 32 years. As a Doctorate Nurse Practitioner (DNP) graduate from Arizona State University, Dr. Beverly practices family medicine as an independent practitioner. She has a specialized interest in adolescent mental health and her specialty is providing medical care for the entire family (newborns to the elderly). Dr. Beverly has also completed advanced practice medical fellowships in pain management and pediatric pulmonary diseases. Dr. Beverly was also a part-time professor teaching students to complete their degrees in the medical profession.

Del Vegara has been caring for patients in the medical field for 30 years. She is a Family Nurse Practitioner graduate from University Of Phoenix. Del practices family medicine as an independent practitioner. She has a specialized interest in urgent care issues, and caring for adolescents and adults. She is licensed to care for the entire family (newborns to elderly).

Both providers have an evidenced based approach to healing. This means our clients get faster and more accurate diagnoses along with the latest and most successful treatments. The bottom-line is: our patients get healthier sooner.
